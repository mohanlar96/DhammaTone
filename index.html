Ringing Time Table (10 Day Course)

Bell goes off on the following timetable except some changes on Day 4 and 10.

Time No of Bell
4:00 a.m. 7 times
4:20 a.m. 5 times
6:30 a.m. 1 time
7:50 a.m. 5 times
9:05 a.m. 3 times
11:00 a.m. 1 times
12:50 p.m. 5 times
2:15 p.m. 1 times
3:35 p.m. 3 times
5:00 p.m. 1 time
5:50 p.m. 5 times

Day 4 afternoon will ring similary but only 2 time table will be different: such as 1:45 -1 time and 1:50 - 3 times.

Day 10 morning differences are 7:50 - 3 times and 9:05 - 3 times.

Day 10 afternoon will play only 2:20 - 3 times and 5:50 - 5 times.
